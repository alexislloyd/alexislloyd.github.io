Alexis Lloyd

Alexis is a product design and innovation leader who designs experiences for how we create and consume information on the internet. She is currently VP of Product Design at Medium and has previously led design and futures work at The New York Times R&D Lab, Axios, and Automattic. She co-founded the Ethical Futures Lab and is passionate about building thoughtful interactions between humans and machines.
