Yellen Signals Rate Increase (Again)

“I believe the case for an increase in the federal funds rate has strengthened in recent months,” is the one quote from Janet Yellen’s speech in Jackson Hole anybody is reading.

Obama’s former economic advisor Austan Goolsbee tweets the Yellen remarks echo “Fed comments from 2015, 2014, 2013, 2012....” Prediction markets agree: Reuters says rate hike probabilities back to where they were before speech; 28% for Sept, 70% for Dec.

Stocks went down then up a bit, bond yields went up then down a bit, the dollar flat; enjoy the weather in Wyoming everybody!

Apple shares are down 2% in Europe; remember it’s one of the richest companies on earth with more than $60b in cash and short-term investments. U.S. Treasury has said the EU actions could “reverse international progress” and Congress could respond with retaliatory taxes.

JP Morgan Chase Spends 125M On Poor Communities

JP Morgan, through its not-for-profit Partnership for Raising Opportunities, will spend the money to revitalize urban communities.

The money will be spent on:

  • Bettering affordable housing options
  • Helping local business thrive
  • Community service projects
  • Healthy food
  • Providing access to education
  • Banishing blight

The program gets results: the bank's 33m investment attracted 226m from other investors, according to Harvard.
