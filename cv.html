Alexis Lloyd

About

Alexis is an award-winning creative director, user experience designer, and researcher whose work focuses on exploring the newly available possibilities of emerging technologies. She designs and creates compelling experiences that demonstrate innovative ways of engaging with information and with the world around us, including interactions with connected objects, data visualizations, and screen-based interfaces. She is also an accomplished writer and public speaker who can convene thoughtful conversations amongst diverse groups about the future of design, technology, and user experience. Her work has been covered and presented internationally in major publications, at prominent conferences, and in galleries and museums.

Experience

Creative Director

The New York Times R&D Lab | 2013 - present

Led a team of creative technologists in the creation of innovative experience prototypes exploring the future of media. Developed a multi-faceted research agenda and synthesized clear points of view on the emerging media and technology landscape. Communicated the outcomes of the lab's research to groups throughout The New York Times as well as to a broader audience through extensive public speaking and writing. During the tenure of my leadership, the lab has become internationally renowned and the projects I led have been covered in numerous publications, including WIRED, The Atlantic, Make Magazine, Le Monde, The Awl, Columbia Journalism Review, and more. The lab also received a 2015 Silver Medal for Best Innovation Program at the Corporate Entrepreneurs Awards. Project portfolio available at www.nytlabs.com.

Creative Technologist

Responsible for researching technology trends and creating award-winning prototypes of future interfaces for news and media consumption. Led user experience and design for the lab as a whole. Prototype development entailed concept creation as well as visual design and front-end programming. Other responsibilities included giving presentations on future visions for technology, news, and content to a wide variety of audiences, as well as writing white papers and articles on current technology topics. Co-Creator and Designer

Responsible for concept development and visual and interaction design for News.me, a personalized news reading app for the iPad. News.me was initially developed as a prototype at The New York Times R+D Lab, and was subsequently sold to bit.ly / betaworks. Engaged in a 6-month residency at bit.ly to design and launch the first version of the News.me app.
