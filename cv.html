Hello.

I'm a creative director, futurist, and technologist. Basically, that means I work to understand how people and technology are evolving alongside each other. I create innovative user experiences that explore emerging possibilities for how we engage with information and with the world around us. My work has been covered and presented internationally in major publications, at prominent conferences, and in galleries and museums.
